Tuscan Chicken Soup

Description

This Tuscan Chicken Soup is a hearty and flavorful dish that combines tender chicken, vegetables, and Italian herbs in a rich broth. Perfect for a comforting meal any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups fresh spinach or kale
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
    2. Add diced chicken and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
    3. Add onion, carrots, celery, and garlic; saut√© until vegetables are tender, about 5 minutes.
    4. Pour in chicken broth and diced tomatoes; stir in oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
    5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
    6. Add spinach or kale and cook for an additional 5 minutes until wilted.
    7. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
